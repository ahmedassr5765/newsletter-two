H.E. Dalia Khorshid's Prominent Participation at the Forbes Women's Summit 2024

At the Forbes Women's Summit 2024, Dr. Dalia Khorshid emphasized the importance of a clear vision, strong leadership, and robust governance in assessing a company's investment readiness.

Dr. Dalia Khorshid highlighted financial health and competitive advantages as crucial indicators, along with alignment to emerging trends like digitization.

The Group CEO & Managing Director also discussed the importance of adaptability and data driven decision-making in managing investments and underscored the need for entrepreneurs to demonstrate transparency, financial discipline, and a deep understanding of their market to attract and maintain investor trust.

At the conclusion of the summit, H.H. Princess Doaa Bint Mohammed, CEO of Al Mohra Education Company, and Khuloud Al Omian, CEO and Editor-in-Chief of Forbes Middle East, honored H.E. Dalia Khorshid to celebrate Her Excellency's exceptional leadership, contributions to the financial sector, and her impact on empowering women in business and entrepreneurship.

Beltone Leasing & Factoring Provides Strategic Financial Arrangement to SEKEM
 

Beltone Leasing and Factoring has entered into a sale and leaseback agreement worth EGP350 million with SEKEM Group, a prominent social enterprise in Egypt dedicated to sustainable development and organic agriculture. This strategic financial arrangement involves the sale and subsequent leaseback of real estate assets, aimed at bolstering SEKEM Group's working capital to support its expansion and enhance its financial capabilities.
